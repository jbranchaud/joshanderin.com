Josh and Erin

"Even after all this time the Sun never says to the Earth, 'You owe me.' Look what happens with a love like that: it lights the whole sky." -Hafiz

Josh and Erin are native Nebraskans who cannot seem to satiate their passion for global travel and new cultural experiences. With a zeal for intentional community, they are living in Argentina for the next year to serve as YAGM. There they will share in daily work and life with the people of Grand Bourg in the Greater Buenos Aires Area. They look forward to being changed by their experiences and hope you will join them through their writings and photos in this blog.
